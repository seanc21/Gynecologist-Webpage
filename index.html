TREAT AND DIAGNOSE DISEASES OF THE FEMALE ORGANS

A Obstetrician deals with all aspects of pregnancy, from prenatal care to post-natal care. An obstetrician can also provide therapies to help you get pregnant, such as fertility treatments.


A Gynecologist is a doctor who specializes in female reproductive health. They diagnose and treat issues related to the female reproductive tract. This includes the uterus, fallopian tubes, and ovaries and breasts.
